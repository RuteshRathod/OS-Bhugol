# Source Documents — Jalna City Municipal Corporation

Place official ward delimitation maps (PDFs/images) in this folder.

## Where to Find Source Maps

1. **JCMC Official Website**: [jcmcjalna.org](https://jcmcjalna.org)
   - Navigate to **"General Election 2025"** or **"Election Department"**
   - Look for **"Final Ward Formation" (अंतिम प्रभाग रचना)**
   - Download individual ward (prabhag) PDFs

2. **Jalna District Portal**: [jalna.gov.in](https://jalna.gov.in)
   - Cross-references election and delimitation data

## Naming Convention

Follow the pattern from Parbhani:
- `mahanagarpalika-Ward-01.jpg` / `.pdf`
- `mahanagarpalika-Ward-02.jpg` / `.pdf`
- ...
- `mahanagarpalika-Ward-16.jpg` / `.pdf`
- `prabhag_rachna_map.jpg` — Overall ward formation map (if available)
